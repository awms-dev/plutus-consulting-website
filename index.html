Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and any information you voluntarily provide about your business. When you become a client, we access financial data (bank transactions, account balances) solely for the purpose of providing bookkeeping services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide bookkeeping services, respond to inquiries, and communicate about your account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial information with third parties except as required to deliver our services (e.g., QuickBooks Online for accounting).

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections, secure cloud-based accounting platforms, and limited access controls. Financial data is stored within QuickBooks Online, which maintains SOC 1 and SOC 2 compliance.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools including QuickBooks Online, Formspree (contact form), and AI-powered categorization tools. Each operates under their own privacy policies. We select vendors that meet industry security standards.
